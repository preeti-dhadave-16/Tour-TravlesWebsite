India Climate & Season

India experiences a diverse climate and weather conditions due to its vast size and geographical features. The country is characterized by five main seasons: Spring, Summer, Monsoon, Autumn, and Winter. The Summer season, from April to mid-June, brings scorching temperatures, especially in the northern plains.

Visa Information

Obtaining a visa for India is an essential step for foreign travelers planning to visit the country. India offers various visa categories, including tourist visas, business visas, and e-visas. The tourist visa allows visitors to explore India's rich cultural heritage, historical sites, and natural beauty.
